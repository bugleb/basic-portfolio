About Me

My name is Brian Bugle and I am Senior Software Engineer at Sphere of Influence. I graduated from Duquesne University in 2013 with a BS in Computer Science. Before joining the team at Sphere, I worked as a software engineer for 4 years at Boeing, mainly in a C++ role. Currently I am part of the UI team for a big data analytics project.

I was born and raised in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I'm a huge Pittsburgh sports fan - Steelers and Penguins are my favorites! In free my time outside of work I like to stay active through sports, working out, hiking local trails, etc.
